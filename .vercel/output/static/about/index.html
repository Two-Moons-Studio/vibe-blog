About

Hi, I'm Jess. I spent 12+ years as a Design Director watching developers say "technically not feasible" to perfectly reasonable ideas. Now I'm a Product Director who decided to stop waiting for dev resources.

This blog documents my journey from Figma to functional—learning to build actual products with AI assistance. No prior coding experience, just design thinking and a lot of curiosity.

What You'll Find Here

Honest takes on what it's really like to learn AI-assisted development as a designer. The struggles, the wins, and everything I wish someone had told me when I started.

  • The Setup - What I was trying to build
  • The Struggle - What went hilariously wrong
  • The Solution - What actually worked
  • The Designer Take - How my design brain processed it
  • The PM Take - What this means for shipping products
  • Reality Check - Time, cost, and sanity scores

If you're a designer, PM, or anyone who's ever wanted to build something but felt blocked by technical barriers, this journey might resonate.
